Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipie

  1. Make the Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking powder, and ghee. Add milk slowly to form a soft, smooth dough; let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Shape the Balls: Roll the dough into small, smooth balls without cracks.
  3. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar and water, add cardamom and a few drops of rose water or saffron for flavor.
  4. Fry and Soak: Deep fry the balls on low heat until golden brown, then soak them in warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes.

Rasagulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • lemon juice
  • sugar
  • water

Recipie

  1. Make Chena (Paneer): Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle it, then strain and rinse to get chena. Knead it until smooth.
  2. Shape Balls: Roll the chena into small, crack-free balls.
  3. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil water and sugar, add cardamom (optional) for flavor.
  4. Cook Rasgullas: Add chena balls to boiling syrup, cover, and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes until they double in size.
